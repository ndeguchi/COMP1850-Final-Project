TIM DAVIS

Tim Davis, is originally from Vancouver. He was obsessed with painting since he was little boy and the passion for art has nerver stopped him. He graduated from The Art Institute of Vancouver in 2007. After he graduated, he contributed for a graphic company creating large and small scale of arts for many years. In 2015, he joined my team and has been working with me since then. His arts are creative and dainamic, and has lots of power to make people happy and I am pretty sure that you would like his arts as well.
